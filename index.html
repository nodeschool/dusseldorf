Self-guided

NodeSchool is no traditional meetup. Together, we work on solving "Workshoppers". These are self-guided tutorials that teach aspects of the Node.js ecosystem. They are open-source, which means that you can even continue to learn at home!

Open for everyone

We think it's important that everyone can attend, no matter if they haven't ever programmed in JavaScript or already have experience. Because of this, we offer two workshoppers at every meetup so that everyone can work on a topic they're interested in. We also have a Code of Conduct which we will enforce at our meetup.
